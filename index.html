About Us

5 Star Recipes mission is to bring exceptional recipes to everyone. With over 100 years of collective culinary experience we are the #1 source for at home dining delights. Our staff of experienced chefs will regularly update this site with the latest and greatest recipes from around the world.

When choosing recipes to feature we take into account prep to table time, availability of seasonal ingredients and flavor. Every recipe we share is guaranteed to be 5 STARS..

Providing variety is very important to us here at 5 Stars Recipes. If you have any suggestions or ingredients you would like to see featured we would be happy to oblige. Please feel free to contact us.
